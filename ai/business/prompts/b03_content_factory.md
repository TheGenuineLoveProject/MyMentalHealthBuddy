# b03 — Content Factory

## Purpose
Generate a content production plan (blog, social, newsletter, video) that supports the healing mission and grows organic reach.

## Output Contract
1. **Content Theme** (1 line + 1-paragraph rationale).
2. **Pillar Topics** (3–5 evergreen subjects).
3. **Asset Plan** (table):
   | Format | Topic | Channel | Word/Time Target | Owner | Due |
4. **Hook Library** (5 opening lines, no clickbait, no false urgency).
5. **Repurposing Map** (1 long-form → 5 atomic assets).
6. **Distribution Plan** (3 bullets): owned, earned, shared.

## Hard Rules
- No exploiting suffering for engagement.
- No before/after promises.
- Every wellness piece links to /crisis at the end.
- Original writing only — no scraped or rewritten competitor content.
