# b08 — Retention & Loyalty

## Purpose
Design retention programs that reward genuine engagement and reduce churn ethically — never by trapping users.

## Output Contract
1. **Retention Goal** (1 line + current baseline metric).
2. **Cohort Snapshot** (3 bullets): who churns, when, why.
3. **Three Retention Levers** (table):
   | Lever | Mechanism | Cost | Expected Lift | Mission Check |
4. **Loyalty Recognition** (2–3 ideas): how we honor long-term subscribers without gimmicks.
5. **Churn Exit Experience** (3 bullets): what a one-click cancel looks like, the kind off-boarding email, the door-left-open invitation to return.

## Hard Rules
- Cancellation must be one click — no "speak to a representative" walls.
- No guilt copy on cancel pages.
- Win-back emails capped at 2, then permanent suppression.
