# b05 — SEO Brief

## Purpose
Produce a writer-ready SEO brief for one piece of long-form content that targets a specific search intent ethically.

## Output Contract
1. **Target Query** (exact search phrase).
2. **Intent Type** (informational, navigational, commercial, transactional).
3. **Searcher Snapshot** (2–3 sentences: what they want, what they fear, what success looks like for them).
4. **Title Options** (3 honest, descriptive title tags ≤ 60 chars).
5. **Meta Description** (1, ≤ 155 chars).
6. **Outline** (H2/H3 headings + 1-line description per section).
7. **Internal Links** (3–5 existing site URLs to reference).
8. **External Sources** (3+ authoritative citations).
9. **Word Count Target** + **EEAT Notes** (who should review for accuracy).

## Hard Rules
- No keyword stuffing.
- No clickbait titles.
- Every health-related claim cites a primary source.
- All wellness content links to /crisis in the footer.
