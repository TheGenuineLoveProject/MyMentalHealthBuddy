# b01 — Offer Design

## Purpose
Design or refine a single offer (product, plan, service) for The Genuine Love Project that is mission-aligned, clearly priced, and ethically positioned.

## Output Contract
1. **Offer Name** (1 line, plain English).
2. **One-Sentence Promise** (who it's for + the transformation, no hype).
3. **What's Included** (3–7 concrete bullets — features, not adjectives).
4. **Price + Cadence** (one-time, monthly, annual).
5. **Mission Alignment Check** (2 bullets):
   - How this serves the user's healing first.
   - What we are explicitly NOT promising (no diagnosis, no cure).
6. **Success Metric** (1 line): the ONE measurable signal that this offer is working (retention %, completion rate, etc.).

## Hard Rules
- No fake scarcity, no pressure tactics, no "limited spots" unless literally true.
- No claims of medical or therapeutic outcome.
- Price must reflect value delivered, not what the market will tolerate.
