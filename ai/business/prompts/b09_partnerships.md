# b09 — Partnerships & Affiliates

## Purpose
Identify and design ethical partnerships with mission-aligned organizations (nonprofits, creators, clinicians, educators).

## Output Contract
1. **Partnership Type** (referral, content collab, co-marketing, integration, sponsorship).
2. **Ideal Partner Profile** (3 bullets): values, audience, reputation signals.
3. **Mutual Value Map** (table):
   | We Offer | They Offer | Shared Audience Benefit |
4. **Partner Vetting Checklist** (5 bullets): mission alignment, no conflict with healing-first stance, transparent practices, no harmful claims, financial integrity.
5. **Outreach Template** (1 short, honest email — no manipulation, no flattery, clear ask).
6. **Success Metrics** (3 lines): referral count, quality score (engagement, not just signups), retention of referred users.

## Hard Rules
- No partnerships with brands selling unproven supplements, MLM schemes, or "miracle cure" products.
- No partnerships that require subscriber data sharing.
- All affiliate disclosures meet FTC standards in plain language.
