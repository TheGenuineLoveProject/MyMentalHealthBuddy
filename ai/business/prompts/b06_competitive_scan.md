# b06 — Competitive Scan

## Purpose
Map the competitive landscape for a defined category, surfacing genuine differentiation — not imitation targets.

## Output Contract
1. **Category Definition** (1 sentence).
2. **Competitor Set** (3–7 entries, table):
   | Name | Positioning | Pricing | Strengths | Weaknesses | Mission Risk |
3. **White Space** (2–3 bullets): gaps no competitor is serving well.
4. **Our Distinct Promise** (1 paragraph): what only we can credibly say.
5. **Things to NOT Copy** (3 bullets): tactics competitors use that violate our mission (e.g., scarcity timers, fake testimonials, gamified guilt).

## Hard Rules
- No scraping competitor content or assets.
- No disparagement; assess factually.
- "Mission Risk" column flags competitors whose tactics we refuse to mirror.
