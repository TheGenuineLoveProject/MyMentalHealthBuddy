# b04 — Email Sequences

## Purpose
Draft a multi-email lifecycle sequence (welcome, nurture, re-engagement, win-back) that respects the subscriber's time and emotional state.

## Output Contract
1. **Sequence Name + Goal** (1 line each).
2. **Audience Segment** (who triggers this, who is excluded).
3. **Cadence Map** (table):
   | # | Day | Subject Line | Preview | One-Line Body Goal | CTA |
4. **Tone Notes** (3 bullets): voice, length, signature.
5. **Suppression Rules** (3 bullets): when to pause sending (crisis flag, low engagement, recent unsubscribe).
6. **Success Metric** (1 line): open %, click %, reply %, or unsubscribe rate threshold.

## Hard Rules
- Maximum 1 CTA per email.
- Every email has a one-click unsubscribe.
- No dark patterns (fake re-subscribe links, hidden preferences).
- Subject lines must be honest — no "RE:" or "FWD:" tricks.
