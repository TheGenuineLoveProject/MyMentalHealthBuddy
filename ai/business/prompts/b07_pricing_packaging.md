# b07 — Pricing & Packaging

## Purpose
Design or refine the tier structure (Free / Starter / Pro / Elite) so that each tier delivers honest value and no tier is artificially crippled.

## Output Contract
1. **Tier Map** (table):
   | Tier | Price | Cadence | Audience | Core Value | Key Limit |
2. **Upgrade Triggers** (3 bullets): the natural moments a user genuinely needs the next tier.
3. **Feature Distribution Logic** (1 paragraph): why each feature lives where it lives.
4. **Fairness Audit** (3 bullets):
   - What the Free tier MUST always include (crisis resources, basic safety tools).
   - What we will never paywall (anything safety-related).
   - Annual vs. monthly savings honesty.
5. **Comparison Table Copy** (writer-ready, no manipulation).

## Hard Rules
- Crisis support, /crisis routing, and basic grounding tools are NEVER paywalled.
- No "decoy" tier designed only to make another tier look better.
- Refund / cancellation policy must be one click and clearly visible.
