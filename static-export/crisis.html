Quick Grounding Techniques

If you're feeling overwhelmed, try one of these gentle exercises to help calm your nervous system.

Box Breathing

  1. Breathe in slowly for 4 seconds
  2. Hold your breath for 4 seconds
  3. Exhale slowly for 4 seconds
  4. Hold for 4 seconds
  5. Repeat 4-6 times

5-4-3-2-1 Senses

  1. Name 5 things you can see
  2. Name 4 things you can touch
  3. Name 3 things you can hear
  4. Name 2 things you can smell
  5. Name 1 thing you can taste

Cold Water Reset

  1. Splash cold water on your face
  2. Hold ice cubes in your hands
  3. Run cold water over your wrists
  4. Focus on the sensation

Gentle Reminders
