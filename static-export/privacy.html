Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At The Genuine Love Project, your privacy and emotional safety are our highest priorities. We understand the sensitive nature of mental wellness data and take extensive measures to protect it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, name, and profile details you provide
  • Wellness Data: Mood entries, journal entries, and session logs
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform to improve your experience
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system for technical support

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your wellness experience
  • To improve our AI-powered features and content
  • To send helpful notifications (with your consent)
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud

4. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all personal data
  • Secure, encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis

5. AI & Your Data

Our AI therapy features process your inputs to provide personalized support. Your conversations are:

  • Never sold to third parties
  • Not used for advertising
  • Anonymized for model improvements (opt-out available)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and preferences. Analytics cookies are optional and can be disabled in your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for payment processing (Stripe), analytics, and email delivery. These partners are bound by strict data protection agreements.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at our contact page.
