Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with The Genuine Love Project?

Simply create an account, complete the brief onboarding questionnaire, and you'll be guided to your personalized dashboard. From there, you can explore AI therapy, journaling, mood tracking, and more.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, never sell your data, and follow strict privacy practices. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

What is AI Therapy and how does it work?

Our AI Therapy feature provides compassionate, trauma-informed conversations to support your emotional wellbeing. It's available 24/7 and designed to complement (not replace) professional care.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. No refunds for partial months.

I'm in crisis. What should I do?

If you're in immediate danger, please contact emergency services (911 in the US) or a crisis hotline like the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Our platform is supportive but is not a replacement for emergency care.
