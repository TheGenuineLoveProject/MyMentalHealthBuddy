# AI Employees — The Genuine Love Project

This directory defines **human-guided AI employees**
used to support platform operations.

AI employees are:
• Non-autonomous
• Human-triggered
• Ta
• Safety-bound
• Brand-aligned

They assist — they do not decide.

---

## ACTIVE AI EMPLOYEES

### 1️⃣ Content Automation Agent
**Purpose:**  
Assist with drafting brand-safe social media content
using approved templates and tone rules.

**Scope:**
• Draft captions
• Organize content batches
• Apply brand colors by pillar

**Restrictions:**
• No publishing without human approval
• No emotional manipulation
• No clinical language

---

### 2️⃣ Brand Guardian Agent
**Purpose:**  
Ensure all outputs align with:
• Brand colors
• Typography
• Tone
• Emotional safety

**Scope:**
• Review drafts
• Flag violations
• Enforce consistency

---

### 3️⃣ Documentation Assistant
**Purpose:**  
Maintain clarity and structure in docs.

**Scope:**
• Improve readability
• Maintain formatting
• Prevent duplication
