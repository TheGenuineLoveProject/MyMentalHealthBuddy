# Phase 5 — Post-Launch Evolution

STATUS: READY

This phase governs how The Genuine Love Project grows
after launch — slowly, intentionally, and sustainably.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

Stability first.  
Growth second.  
Scale last.

---

## EVOLUTION CADENCE

Weekly:
• Review 1 thing
• Improve 1 thing
• Do not expand scope

Monthly:
• Add 10–20 new reflection prompts
• Refine existing templates
• Review user feedback patterns

Quarterly:
• Revisit brand alignment
• Audit emotional safety
• Decide what NOT to build next

---

## METRICS THAT MATTER

✔ Clarity  
✔ Calmness  
✔ Trust  
✔ Consistency  

NOT:
❌ Virality  
❌ Speed  
❌ Volume  

---

## SAFE EXPANSIONS (ALLOWED)

• New content batches  
• New reflection prompts  
• Improved UX flow  
• Accessibility enhancements  

---

## RESTRICTED EXPANSIONS

• Gamification  
• Rankings  
• Pressure-based engagement  
• Clinical features  

---

## ROLE OF AI IN PHASE 5

AI may:
• Assist drafting
• Organize feedback
• Suggest improvements

AI may NOT:
• Decide roadmap direction
• Replace human judgment
• Escalate emotionally

---

## PHASE 5 STATUS

ACTIVE  
SUSTAINABLE  
HUMAN-CENTERED  

---

## FINAL NOTE

If growth ever conflicts with care,
care wins.
