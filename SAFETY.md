# Safety & Wellness Boundaries

## Important Disclaimer

**The Genuine Love Project is NOT a medical or mental health service.**

This platform provides:
- Self-reflection and personal growth tools
- Evidence-informed wellness practices
- Compassionate AI support for emotional processing
- Educational content about mental wellness
- Connection to professional resources when needed

This platform does NOT:
- Diagnose or treat mental health conditions
- Provide clinical therapy or professional counseling
- Replace licensed healthcare providers
- Guarantee specific outcomes or results

## Crisis Resources

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please contact emergency services immediately.

### 24/7 Crisis Hotlines

| Service | Contact | Details |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** | Call or text **988** | Free, confidential support 24/7 |
| **Crisis Text Line** | Text **HOME** to **741741** | Text-based crisis support |
| **Emergency Services** | Call **911** | Immediate danger |

### Additional Resources

- **SAMHSA National Helpline**: 1-800-662-4357 (treatment referrals)
- **NAMI Helpline**: 1-800-950-NAMI (mental health support)
- **Veterans Crisis Line**: 988 then press 1
- **Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ Youth)**: 1-866-488-7386

## When to Seek Professional Help

We encourage you to seek professional support if you experience:

- Persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, or emptiness
- Thoughts of self-harm or harming others
- Significant changes in sleep, appetite, or daily functioning
- Trauma symptoms that interfere with your quality of life
- Substance use concerns
- Relationship difficulties that feel overwhelming

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a supportive environment that respects your journey. Your safety and wellbeing are our top priorities. If at any point you feel this platform is not serving your needs, we encourage you to seek support that aligns with your path to healing.
