# External Reviewer Feedback Collection

STATUS: ACTIVE

---

## PURPOSE

Collect structured, non-disruptive feedback
from external reviewers without changing system state.

---

## FEEDBACK SCOPE

Reviewers may comment on:
• Clarity
• Calmness
• Ethical alignment
• UX safety
• Documentation understanding

Reviewers may NOT propose:
❌ Feature additions
❌ Growth tactics
❌ Monetization pressure
❌ Engagement mechanics

---

## FEEDBACK FORMAT (REQUIRED)

Reviewers must submit feedback using:

1. What feels clear?
2. What feels confusing?
3. Where does the system feel calm?
4. Where does it feel heavy?
5. Any ethical concerns?

---

## COLLECTION RULES

• Feedback is written only
• No live changes
• No immediate action
• Advisory status only

---

## REVIEW PROCESS

• Founder reviews feedback
• Patterns are noted
• No action taken without explicit unlock

---

## OUTPUT

A summarized insights document may be created
after all feedback is reviewed.

---

## STEWARDSHIP

All feedback is optional.
Care remains the priority.
