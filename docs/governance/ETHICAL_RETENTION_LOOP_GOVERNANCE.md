# Ethical Retention Loop Governance

## Purpose
Create return-user continuity without manipulation, addiction mechanics, fear, urgency, shame, or emotional dependency.

## Core Law
Retention must mean genuine user value, not coercive engagement.

## Allowed Retention Loops
- saved reflections
- continue your journey
- gentle check-ins
- saved tools
- weekly grounding
- unfinished guided journeys
- user-controlled reminders
- opt-in newsletters
- progress without pressure

## Forbidden Patterns
- manipulative streaks
- shame reminders
- urgency pressure
- fear-based notifications
- hidden opt-ins
- emotional dependency loops
- healing-data monetization
- vulnerability targeting
