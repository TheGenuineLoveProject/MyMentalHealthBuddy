# Self-Improving Platform Governance

Purpose:
Build MyMentalHealthBuddy as a safe, compassionate, human-supervised, evidence-informed, problem-solving platform for user wellbeing, clarity, self-understanding, emotional intelligence, and ethical growth.

Non-negotiables:
- Human safety first.
- Licensed provider oversight for clinical domains.
- No AI diagnosis, prescribing, crisis determination, or replacement of emergency services.
- No exploitation of emotional vulnerability.
- No dark patterns.
- No healing-data monetization.
- No use of private emotional content for ads, pricing, retargeting, or manipulation.
- Improvements must be verified before being claimed complete.

Self-improvement loop:
1. Detect issue.
2. Classify risk.
3. Apply smallest safe fix.
4. Verify build, health, readiness, routes, security, and revenue flow.
5. Log evidence.
6. Stop before expanding scope.

Platform capability goals:
- Help users understand themselves clearly.
- Provide compassionate tools, reflections, education, and guided practices.
- Support provider-reviewed wellness pathways.
- Strengthen emotional intelligence, self-awareness, values alignment, and resilience.
- Keep revenue systems separate from private healing content.
- Make subscription value visible through practical tools, structured journeys, dashboards, and safe human support.

Revenue principles:
- Sell clarity, structure, support, convenience, guided growth, premium tools, and provider-informed experiences.
- Do not sell fear, dependency, diagnosis, false certainty, or spiritual/clinical superiority.
- Subscription value must be understandable within 30 seconds on pricing and premium pages.

Current operating rule:
One verified blocker at a time.
