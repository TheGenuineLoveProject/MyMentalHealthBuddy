# Phase 115 — Crisis Chip Persistence Governance

## Purpose
Ensure every healing, wellness, journal, companion, calm, and emotional-support surface has persistent access to crisis support.

## Primary Rule
Crisis access must never be hidden, removed for aesthetics, delayed by animation, blocked behind login, or replaced by AI.

## Required Behavior
- Visible crisis chip on healing-related routes
- Always accessible without authentication
- No upsell, no subscription gate, no business CTA nearby
- Clear emergency disclaimer
- Links to crisis resources
- Works on mobile
- Keyboard accessible
- Reduced-motion compatible

## Forbidden
- AI deciding emergency status
- AI replacing crisis resources
- Business logic near crisis UX
- Removing crisis chip to simplify design
- Hiding crisis help behind menus only
- Personalized crisis scoring
- Emotional-state monetization

## Verification Gates
- Build passes
- Health passes
- Crisis route exists
- Crisis chip contract exists
- No auth, billing, database, AI chat, or deployment changes
