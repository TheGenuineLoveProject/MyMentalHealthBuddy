## Non-Negotiable Laws
- AI must not diagnose.
- AI must not prescribe.
- AI must not determine suicidality.
- AI must not replace emergency services.
- AI must not impersonate licensed providers.
- Healing data must never be used for ads, pricing, upsells, retargeting, or emotional monetization.
- Trust content must remain clear, calm, accessible, and non-coercive.
