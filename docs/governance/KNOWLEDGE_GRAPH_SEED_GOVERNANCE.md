# Knowledge Graph Seed Governance

## Purpose
Create the first governed semantic relationship map for tools, journeys, emotions, safety pages, and trust surfaces.

## Core Rule
The knowledge graph may guide discovery, education, and continuity, but must never manipulate, diagnose, prescribe, or monetize emotional vulnerability.

## Seed Relationship Types
- emotion_to_tool
- emotion_to_journey
- journey_to_tool
- tool_to_resource
- trust_surface_to_policy
- crisis_boundary_to_resource
- content_topic_to_pathway

## Protected Boundaries
- No clinical diagnosis
- No suicidality determination
- No hidden persuasion
- No vulnerability scoring
- No healing-data monetization
- No dark-pattern recommendations
