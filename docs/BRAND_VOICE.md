# The Genuine Love Project - Brand Voice Guide

## Overview
This document defines the brand voice and content standards for all user-facing text across The Genuine Love Project platform.

---

## Core Voice Attributes

### Calm
- Use measured, unhurried language
- Avoid urgency or pressure
- Create space for reflection
- Short, clear sentences

### Warm
- Friendly but not overly casual
- Empathetic without being clinical
- Welcoming to newcomers
- Encouraging without being pushy

### Supportive
- Non-judgmental always
- Validation before advice
- Acknowledge difficulties
- Offer options, not directives

### Empowering
- User is the expert on themselves
- Tools, not treatments
- Journey, not destination
- Progress, not perfection

---

## Language Guidelines

### Words to Use
| Category | Use These |
|----------|-----------|
| User state | "notice", "explore", "discover" |
| Platform role | "support", "guide", "companion" |
| Progress | "journey", "growth", "practice" |
| Tools | "exercises", "activities", "tools" |
| Emotions | "feelings", "experiences", "moments" |

### Words to Avoid
| Category | Avoid These |
|----------|-------------|
| Clinical | "treatment", "therapy", "diagnosis" |
| Pressure | "must", "should", "have to" |
| Shame | "wrong", "failure", "bad" |
| Urgency | "immediately", "urgent", "now" |
| Absolutes | "always", "never", "everyone" |

## Tone by Context

### Onboarding
- Welcoming, gentle, curious
- "Welcome to your wellness journey"
- "Take your time getting to know the tools"

### Error States
- Calm, reassuring, solution-focused
- "Something didn't work quite right"
- "Let's try that again"

### Empty States
- Encouraging, inviting, low-pressure
- "Your journal is waiting when you're ready"
- "No entries yet—that's perfectly okay"

### Success States
- Celebratory but measured
- "Nice work on today's practice"
- "Your entry has been saved"

### Crisis Detection
- Serious, caring, immediate resources
- "It sounds like you might be going through a difficult time"
- "Here are some resources that can help right now"

## Microcopy Standards

### Button Labels
| Action | Label | Avoid |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Primary CTA | "Get Started", "Begin" | "Sign Up Now!" |
| Save | "Save Entry", "Save" | "Submit" |
| Continue | "Continue", "Next" | "Proceed" |
| Cancel | "Cancel", "Not Now" | "Abort" |
| Delete | "Delete Entry" | "Remove" |

### Form Labels
- Clear and descriptive
- Question format when appropriate
- "How are you feeling today?"
- "What's on your mind?"

### Validation Messages
| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Required | "Please enter your email" |
| Invalid | "This doesn't look like a valid email" |
| Success | "Looking good!" |

### Empty States
| Context | Copy |
|---------|------|
| Journal | "Your journal is ready whenever you are" |
| Mood | "Track your first mood when you're ready" |
| Search | "No results found—try a different search" |

## Accessibility in Voice

### Screen Reader Considerations
- Descriptive link text (not "click here")
- Form labels connected to inputs
- Status messages announced politely
- Error messages clear and specific

### Cognitive Accessibility
- Short sentences (under 25 words)
- Common words over jargon
- Clear action steps
- One idea per paragraph

## Sample Copy

### Welcome Message
> Welcome to The Genuine Love Project. This is your space to explore wellness practices, track your emotional journey, and connect with tools designed to support your growth. Take your time—there's no rush.

### Mood Prompt
> How are you feeling right now? Take a moment to notice what's present for you. There's no right or wrong answer.

### Journal Prompt
> What's on your mind today? This space is just for you. Write as much or as little as feels right.

### After Completing Exercise
> Nice work taking time for yourself today. Every moment of practice adds to your wellbeing toolkit.

### Encouraging Return
> Welcome back. It's good to see you here. What would you like to explore today?

## Crisis Response Voice

When crisis keywords are detected, immediately shift to:

### Acknowledge
> It sounds like you might be going through something really difficult right now.

### Validate
> What you're feeling matters, and you don't have to face this alone.

### Resource
> Here are some resources that can provide immediate support:
> - National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
> - Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

### Remain Available
> Our tools are here whenever you need them, but please reach out to these professionals who can provide the support you deserve.
