# Privacy Policy

## Overview

The Genuine Love Project ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Display name (optional)
- Authentication data (hashed passwords)

### Usage Data
- Journal entries (encrypted at rest)
- Mood tracking data
- Session activity logs
- AI conversation history

### Technical Data
- IP addresses (for security)
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Usage analytics

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Provide Services**: Power journaling, mood tracking, and AI features
2. **Improve Platform**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality
3. **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
4. **Communication**: Send account-related notifications (opt-in for marketing)

## Data Protection

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Sensitive data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Passwords hashed with bcrypt
- Regular security audits and monitoring

## Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Journal entries: User-controlled deletion
- Logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes
- Deleted accounts: Data purged within 30 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request data correction
- Request data deletion
- Export your data
- Opt out of marketing communications

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Stripe**: Payment processing (PCI compliant)
- **OpenAI**: AI features (data not used for training)
- **Sentry**: Error monitoring (anonymized)

For privacy inquiries, contact us at: privacy@genuineloveproject.com
