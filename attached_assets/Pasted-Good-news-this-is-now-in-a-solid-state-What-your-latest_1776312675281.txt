	•	meta.status

Recommendation 3 — add canonicalUrl finalization rule

Right now canonicalUrl can remain null on generated draft-like content. That is okay for draft generation, but before publish you should enforce:
	•	category present
	•	canonicalUrl derived or explicit
	•	seo valid

That belongs in the next contract layer, not this patch.
