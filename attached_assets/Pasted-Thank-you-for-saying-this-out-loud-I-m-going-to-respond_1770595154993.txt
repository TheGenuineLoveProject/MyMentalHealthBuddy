First: what dissociation actually is (plain truth)

Dissociation is the body putting distance between you and overload.
It happens when the nervous system decides:

“This is too much to process right now.”

So it shifts you into:
	•	fog
	•	detachment
	•	unreal / floaty feeling
	•	difficulty thinking or integrating ideas
	•	feeling split between mind and body

This is not weakness.
It’s an automatic survival response, especially common after long-term stress or trauma.
