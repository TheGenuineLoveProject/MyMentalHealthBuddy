# CRM Integration Plan

## CRM Goal
Capture interested users ethically and guide them into the correct next step.

## Lead Stages
1. Visitor
2. Free reflection user
3. Email subscriber
4. 7-Day Reset buyer
5. Monthly subscriber
6. Returning growth member

## Required Fields
- email
- consent timestamp
- source page
- selected interest
- offer clicked
- purchase status

## Ethical Rules
- no emotional vulnerability scoring
- no crisis retargeting
- no manipulation
- no hidden personalization based on private healing data
- easy unsubscribe
