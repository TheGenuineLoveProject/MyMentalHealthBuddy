2. Email subscriber
3. Free reflection user
4. 7-day reset customer
5. Monthly subscriber
6. Returning member

- welcome email
- reminder email
- reflection streak email
- abandoned checkout email
- weekly growth summary
- re-engagement sequence

- ConvertKit or MailerLite
- Stripe
- PostHog
- Plausible
- Resend
