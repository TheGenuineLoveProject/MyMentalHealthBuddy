# Provider-Guided Revenue Model

## Provider Role
Licensed providers help review educational content, safety boundaries, escalation language, and wellness guidance.

## Provider Value
- increases trust
- improves safety
- supports evidence-informed education
- strengthens ethical positioning

## Boundaries
The platform does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, or replace therapy.

## Sellable Trust Message
"Provider-informed wellness education with compassionate AI-guided reflection."
