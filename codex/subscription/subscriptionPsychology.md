# Subscription Psychology

## Why Users Subscribe
- emotional relief
- clarity
- self-understanding
- calm
- identity growth
- support
- transformation proof
- healthier relationships
- purpose
- emotional safety

## Why Users Stay
- visible progress
- emotional trust
- practical daily value
- compassionate guidance
- nervous-system-safe experience
- authentic growth
- measurable wellbeing improvement

## Why Users Leave
- manipulation
- overwhelm
- vague spirituality
- fake positivity
- emotional pressure
- addictive engagement loops
- lack of meaningful progress
