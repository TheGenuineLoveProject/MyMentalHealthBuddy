# Ethical Million Dollar Path

## Hard Truth
A million dollars in one month requires scale, distribution, partnerships, strong conversion, and paid acquisition. It cannot be guaranteed.

## Ethical Paths
- subscriptions
- provider-reviewed courses
- workplace wellness packages
- veteran wellness programs
- downloadable workbooks
- premium guided journeys
- community memberships
- licensing to organizations

## Non-Negotiables
No false claims. No fear-based selling. No crisis monetization. No emotional manipulation.
