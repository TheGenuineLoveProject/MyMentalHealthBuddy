# Revenue Reality Plan

## Hard Truth
A million dollars in one month is possible only with major traffic, strong offer-market fit, paid acquisition, partnerships, influencers, enterprise deals, or an existing audience.

## Ethical Revenue Paths
- subscription memberships
- premium guided growth plans
- provider-reviewed courses
- workplace wellness packages
- veteran/community wellness programs
- coaching-adjacent educational products
- downloadable workbooks
- social media funnels

## Do Not Use
- fear-based selling
- false cure claims
- emotional dependency
- manipulation
- fake urgency
- crisis monetization
