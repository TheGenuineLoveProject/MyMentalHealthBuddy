# User Journey

> **Phase:** B1 · **Mode:** GOVERNANCE / POSITIONING ONLY (no runtime code).
> **Governed by:** MMHB v7.4 Archival Kernel. Educational only — no diagnosis. Trauma-informed, consent-based, non-coercive. Crisis routing preserved on every wellness surface.

This document defines the **wellbeing-centered journey** — from first arrival to long-term, self-authored growth — designed to support, not to capture.

---

## 1. Journey law

The journey is **a spiral the person authors**, not a funnel we push them through. Every stage is opt-in, reversible, paced by the person, and free of coercion. Leaving well is a success, not a failure.

---

## 2. Wellbeing-centered onboarding

Onboarding orients toward **safety and consent first**, never data-maximization.

- **Welcome & safety** — warm framing; crisis resources visible immediately (`/crisis`, 988, 741741).
- **Consent-first** — the person chooses what to share; sensitive data (embodiment) is off by default.
- **Gentle, finite first step** — one small, optional action (a feeling named, a breath) — no overwhelming setup.
- **No dark-pattern signup** — no forced continuity, no pre-checked data sharing, easy to skip.
- **Honest expectations** — plainly states what MMHB is (support) and is not (therapy/diagnosis).
- **Accessibility** — WCAG AA, reduced-motion honored from the first screen.

---

## 3. Journey stages (spiral, not funnel)

| Stage | Person's experience | Design stance |
|---|---|---|
| **Arrive** | "Is this safe? Is this for me?" | Safety, consent, honesty, crisis routing |
| **Notice** | First reflections; naming feelings | Gentle, finite, dismissible |
| **Practice** | Returning to tools at own pace | No streak pressure; rest honored |
| **Integrate** | Patterns noticed, small aligned actions | Self-authored reflection (TI) |
| **Rest** | Pausing | Celebrated, neutral, no guilt nudges |
| **Return** | Coming back after a pause | Warm welcome, never penalized |
| **Outgrow** | Needing the app less | Honored — "leaving well" is success |

---

## 4. Long-term transformation journey

- **Spiral depth** — revisiting themes at new depth over months/years, not linear levels.
- **Seasons** — active and resting seasons both valid; reflected neutrally (see `../transformation/transformationTimelineModel.md`).
- **Self-defined milestones** — only the person marks milestones; TI may gently, opt-in, suggest.
- **Graceful off-ramp** — MMHB supports needing it less; no retention-at-all-costs.
- **Continuity of trust** — privacy and respect are constant across the whole journey.

---

## 5. Ethical retention systems

Retention is earned through **genuine value**, never coercion.

- **Allowed:** real usefulness, gentle opt-in invitations, respectful re-entry, trustworthy privacy.
- **Forbidden:** streak-bait, loss-framing, FOMO, guilt notifications, manufactured urgency, hard-to-leave flows, engagement-maximizing AI. (See `../transformation/nonManipulativeEngagement.md`.)
- **Test:** if a retention mechanic would not feel kind to a struggling person, it does not ship.

---

## 6. Crisis on the journey

- Crisis resources are reachable at **every** stage.
- An explicit self-harm signal overrides all journey logic and routes to resources immediately (BHCE): `/crisis`, 988, 741741, 911.
- Crisis is never treated as a "journey metric."
