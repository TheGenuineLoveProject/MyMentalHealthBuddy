# Platform Differentiators

> **Phase:** B1 · **Mode:** GOVERNANCE / POSITIONING ONLY (no runtime code).
> **Governed by:** MMHB v7.4 Archival Kernel. Educational only — no diagnosis. Trauma-informed, consent-based, non-coercive. Business/brand domain — never contaminates healing flows.

This document defines how MMHB is **differentiated** — especially against attention-extractive social media — through a healing-first, anti-manipulation stance.

---

## 1. Differentiation thesis

Most apps are optimized to **capture attention**. MMHB is optimized to **return a person to themselves and then let them go**. Success is the person's wellbeing and agency — not time-on-app, not DAU, not conversion.

---

## 2. Platform differentiation against social media apps

| Dimension | Typical social media | MMHB |
|---|---|---|
| **North-star metric** | Engagement / time-on-app | Person's wellbeing & agency |
| **Feed** | Infinite scroll, algorithmic comparison | No infinite feed; calm, finite, self-paced reflection |
| **Notifications** | Engagement-bait, FOMO, red badges | Opt-in, person-set, invitation-not-pressure |
| **Rewards** | Variable-reward / dopamine loops | No slot-machine mechanics; no streak shame |
| **Comparison** | Likes, followers, leaderboards | No comparison to other people, ever |
| **Data** | Profiling for ad targeting | Private-by-default; reflections are the person's own |
| **Exit** | Friction to leave (roach-motel) | Easy pause/off; "enough for today" is celebrated |
| **AI role** | Maximize engagement | Gentle mirror & librarian; defers to humans |

---

## 3. Healing vs. manipulation differentiation

| | Healing design (MMHB) | Manipulative design (rejected) |
|---|---|---|
| **Intent** | Serve the person's wellbeing | Extract attention/money |
| **Urgency** | None; the person sets pace | Manufactured scarcity/FOMO |
| **Framing** | Compassion, consent, choice | Inadequacy, shame, loss-framing |
| **Reward** | Intrinsic (self-relationship) | Intermittent reinforcement |
| **Transparency** | "Why you're seeing this" in plain language | Hidden persuasion weights |
| **Off-ramp** | Easy, honored | Hard, guilt-laden |
| **Crisis** | Always routed to resources | Ignored or exploited |

> This table is the operational test for any new feature: if it lands on the right column, it does not ship.

---

## 4. Core differentiators (positioning pillars)

1. **Anti-manipulation by design** — no dark patterns, no addiction loops (see `../transformation/nonManipulativeEngagement.md`).
2. **Nervous-system-safe** — calm, paced, reduced-motion, WCAG AA.
3. **Self-authored growth** — the person's words and pace define progress.
4. **Privacy & trust** — private-by-default; never sold or profiled for persuasion.
5. **Crisis-first ethics** — resources always in reach; safety overrides everything.
6. **Honest claims** — educational, non-clinical, confidence-honest; no cure promises.
7. **Genuine Love Project mission** — reduce stigma, increase access, treat people as whole.

---

## 5. Guardrails

- Differentiator messaging is BUSINESS-domain; it never appears inside healing/crisis surfaces (Primary Law).
- No disparagement that manufactures fear; differentiation is stated through MMHB's own standards.
