# Proof of Growth Signals

> **Phase:** B1 · **Mode:** GOVERNANCE / ARCHITECTURE ONLY (no runtime code).
> **Governed by:** MMHB v7.4 Archival Kernel. Educational only — no diagnosis, no clinical proof claims. Consent-gated, reversible, non-coercive. Crisis routing preserved.

This document defines **transformation proof systems** — how MMHB can honestly reflect *evidence of a person's own growth* back to them, without overclaiming, gaming, or manipulating.

---

## 1. Proof law

"Proof" means **honest, self-validated reflection of change** — not clinical evidence, not a guarantee, not a marketing claim about a person. The person is the final authority on whether they've grown. Proof is confidence-honest and correctable.

---

## 2. Transformation proof systems

- **Self-validation primacy** — the strongest proof is the person's own "yes, this changed for me." TI mirrors; the person confirms.
- **Then-and-now contrast** — opt-in reflection comparing the person's own words across time (`transformationTimelineModel.md`).
- **Pattern-to-action** — noticing a pattern, then a small aligned action, reflected as a moment of growth.
- **Tool-supported regulation** — self-noted softening after using grounding/breathing tools.
- **Returning after rest** — re-entry honored as part of the story (proof of resilience, not "you lapsed").

> Proof moments are **self-set or opt-in suggested** (dismissible), never auto-asserted by the system.

---

## 3. Evidence-informed growth indicators

Indicators reference recognized, **non-clinical** wellness concepts and are documented as *informational*, never validated assessment:

| Indicator | Concept basis (non-clinical) | Confidence handling |
|---|---|---|
| Emotion-naming range | affect labeling / emotional granularity | sparse → softer claim |
| Reframing in entries | cognitive reframing (wellness sense) | corroborated across entries |
| Time-to-calm | self-soothing / window of tolerance | self-reported only |
| Self-compassion language | self-compassion practice | mirror, not measure |
| Aligned micro-actions | values-based action | person-confirmed |

**No false precision:** qualitative bands (early / emerging / consistent), no numeric certainty, no normative comparison.

---

## 4. Authenticity metrics (anti-overclaiming)

- **Correction loop** — "this doesn't fit" suppresses and down-weights a reflection.
- **Sparse-data honesty** — fewer/softer reflections when data is thin ("it's early").
- **No proxy gaming** — indicators may never be tuned to inflate engagement, retention, or conversion.
- **Provenance** — every indicator traces to a defined, consented signal (`transformationSignals.md`).
- **Self-validation over system-assertion** — the system never claims growth the person hasn't confirmed.

---

## 5. Healing vs. manipulation (proof-specific)

| Honest proof (MMHB) | Manipulative "proof" (rejected) |
|---|---|
| Person confirms their own growth | System asserts growth to drive engagement |
| Confidence-honest, correctable | Overclaimed, false precision |
| Private, person-only | Used for marketing/persuasion |
| Rest/return honored | "You lapsed" loss-framing |
| No comparison | Ranking vs. others |

---

## 6. Guardrails

- Educational only; no clinical proof or outcome guarantee.
- Consent-gated, reversible, private; WCAG AA.
- No business content on proof surfaces (Primary Law).
- Crisis routing present; distress never framed as "proof" data.
