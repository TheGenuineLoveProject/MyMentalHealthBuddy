# Transformation Signals

> **Phase:** T1 · **Mode:** GOVERNANCE / ARCHITECTURE ONLY (no runtime code).
> **Governed by:** MMHB v7.4 Archival Kernel. Educational only — no diagnosis, no clinical automation. Consent-gated, reversible, non-coercive.

This document defines the **observable signals** TI may derive (from data the person already chose to record) to support gentle self-reflection. Signals are **descriptive inputs**, not verdicts. Every signal is opt-in, reversible, and crisis-deferential.

---

## 1. Signal principles

- **Evidence-informed, not diagnostic** — signals draw on well-established, non-clinical wellness concepts (emotion naming, window of tolerance, self-compassion, gratitude, restorative practice). They never produce a clinical label or score-of-self.
- **Consent-gated** — a signal is only computed if the person opted into that data category.
- **Transparent** — every reflection can show "why you're seeing this" in plain language.
- **Low-confidence-honest** — sparse data yields *fewer/softer* reflections, never confident claims. TI says "it's early" rather than guessing.
- **Crisis-deferential** — distress signals never become a "score"; explicit risk routes to crisis (BHCE), full stop.

---

## 2. Growth indicators (descriptive)

Signals that someone is *noticing and integrating* over time. Always framed as the person's own movement, never as pass/fail.

- **Frequency of self-reflection** — cadence of journaling/check-ins (presence, not pressure).
- **Vocabulary of feeling** — growth in range/specificity of named emotions.
- **Reframing** — entries that revisit a difficulty with new perspective.
- **Pattern recognition** — the person naming their own recurring patterns.
- **Aligned micro-actions** — small actions consistent with their stated values.

> Anti-streak rule: growth indicators are **never** rendered as breakable streaks, counters that shame, or "don't lose your progress" mechanics.

---

## 3. Reflection intelligence

How TI reads *qualitative* reflection (the person's own words), gently and privately.

- **Theme surfacing** — recurring themes the person can choose to explore ("rest" or "boundaries" keep appearing).
- **Self-authored meaning** — TI mirrors the person's own framings back; it does not impose interpretations.
- **Contrast over time** — optional, opt-in "then vs. now" reflections in the person's own words.
- **Privacy-first** — reflection content stays in the person's own scope; no sharing, no profiling for any non-reflective purpose.
- **Right to be unread** — the person can exclude any entry from reflection at any time.

---

## 4. Emotional regulation indicators

Descriptive cues about returning to balance — never a measure of "stability worth."

- **Time-to-calm** — self-noted softening after distress (e.g., after using a grounding tool).
- **Window of tolerance** — gentle reflection on range the person can stay present with.
- **Tool engagement** — voluntary use of breathing/grounding/self-compassion practices.
- **Co-regulation cues** — opt-in noting of reaching out or feeling supported.

**Hard rule:** regulation indicators must never be used to gate features, nudge purchases, or imply the person is "not ready." Distress is met with **support and resources**, never scoring.

---

## 5. Embodiment metrics

Felt-sense and nervous-system signals (Domain D4), strictly self-reported and optional.

- **Rest & restoration** — self-logged rest, sleep quality reflections (descriptive).
- **Breath & grounding** — voluntary practice engagement.
- **Body cues** — opt-in noting of body signals (tension, ease) the person chooses to track.
- **Pacing** — honoring "enough for today"; TI actively supports stopping.

> Embodiment data is the most sensitive; default is **off**. Nothing here is medical measurement.

---

## 6. Evidence-informed measurement

- **Concept provenance** — every signal references a recognized, non-clinical wellness concept and is documented as *informational*, not validated assessment.
- **No false precision** — no decimal "accuracy," no clinical thresholds, no normative comparison to other people.
- **Confidence bands** — reflections carry qualitative confidence (early / emerging / consistent), never a numeric certainty.
- **Falsifiable & correctable** — the person can mark any reflection "this doesn't fit," which suppresses and down-weights it.
- **Auditability** — signal definitions live in these docs; any future computation must trace back to a defined, consented signal.
