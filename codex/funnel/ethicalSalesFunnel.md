# Ethical Sales Funnel

## Awareness
User sees content about emotional clarity, self-awareness, and healing.

## Trust
User receives free prompts, education, and emotionally safe guidance.

## Conversion
User subscribes because the platform helps them understand themselves.

## Retention
User stays because they see progress, calm, consistency, and self-trust.

## Ethical Rules
- no fear pressure
- no shame
- no false urgency
- no dependency loops
- no cure claims
- no exploitation of pain
