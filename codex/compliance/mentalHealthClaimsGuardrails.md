# Mental Health Claims Guardrails

## Allowed
- may support wellbeing
- may help users reflect
- may improve self-awareness
- educational wellness tools
- provider-informed content

## Not Allowed
- cures depression
- treats trauma
- replaces therapy
- diagnoses users
- guarantees transformation
- prevents crisis
- clinically monitors without provider workflow

## Required Language
This platform provides educational wellness support and is not a substitute for professional mental health care, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services.
