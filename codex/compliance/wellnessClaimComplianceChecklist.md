# Wellness Claim Compliance Checklist

## Allowed Language
- support
- guide
- reflect
- learn
- practice
- grow
- understand
- calm
- build awareness

## Avoid Language
- cure
- diagnose
- treat
- guaranteed healing
- replace therapy
- emergency support
- clinically proven unless cited
- provider replacement

## Required Boundary
This platform provides wellness education and guided self-reflection. It is not therapy, diagnosis, medical treatment, or crisis care.
