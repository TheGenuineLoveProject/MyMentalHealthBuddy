# SEO CRM Social Compliance

## Every Public Claim Must Be
- educational
- non-clinical unless reviewed
- non-diagnostic
- non-coercive
- non-guaranteed
- wellness-centered

## Provider Review Required For
- mental health education
- crisis-adjacent language
- trauma-related content
- clinical terminology
- outcome claims
- safety disclaimers

## Business-Healing Firewall
Private healing data, journal entries, crisis content, provider notes, and emotional vulnerability indicators must never be used for ads, pricing, targeting, upsells, segmentation, or manipulative retention.
