# Provider Monitoring Architecture

## Purpose
Licensed providers guide safety, quality, boundaries, and evidence-informed wellness content.

## Provider Oversight Areas
- psychoeducation review
- AI boundary review
- crisis language review
- escalation policy review
- wellness content quality
- non-diagnostic safeguards
- ethical transformation language

## Boundary
Providers supervise quality and safety. AI never diagnoses, treats, prescribes, or replaces emergency care.
