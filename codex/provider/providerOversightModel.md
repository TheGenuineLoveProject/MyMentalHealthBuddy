# Provider Oversight Model

## Purpose
Licensed mental health providers guide safety, content review, escalation standards, evidence alignment, and ethical platform development.

## Provider Roles
- review wellness content
- approve clinical boundary language
- monitor risk pathways
- improve psychoeducation quality
- audit AI outputs
- protect users from harm
- prevent diagnosis/treatment/cure claims

## AI Boundary
AI supports education, reflection, and wellness guidance only. Licensed providers retain clinical authority.
