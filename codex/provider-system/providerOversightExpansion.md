# Provider Oversight Expansion

## Role of Licensed Providers
Providers:
- review educational systems
- improve emotional safety
- monitor harmful UX patterns
- validate wellness frameworks
- improve escalation governance

## Providers Do Not
- diagnose through AI
- replace therapy
- provide autonomous treatment
- create dependency systems

## Human Supervision Goals
- emotional safety
- evidence-informed guidance
- ethical platform evolution
- trust reinforcement

## Platform Trust Advantage
The platform combines:
- compassionate AI systems
- structured self-reflection
- licensed provider oversight
- measurable emotional growth
