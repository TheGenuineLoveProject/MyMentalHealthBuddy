# Ethical Conversion Architecture

## Users Subscribe For
- emotional support
- structure
- reflection
- progress tracking
- self-understanding
- consistency
- emotional clarity

## Ethical Retention
Retention should come from:
- usefulness
- trust
- measurable growth
- emotional safety
- progress visibility

## Never Use
- shame
- fear
- manipulation
- emotional dependency
- false urgency
- fake scarcity

## Core Conversion Trigger
Users feel:
"This platform genuinely helps me understand myself."
