# Sellable Transformation Model

## Core Truth
Users do not subscribe to abstract enlightenment. Users subscribe to relief, clarity, safety, self-understanding, and visible growth.

## What We Sell Ethically
- emotional clarity
- nervous-system calm
- self-awareness
- authentic identity growth
- relationship insight
- purpose alignment
- guided reflection
- measurable wellbeing progress

## What We Do Not Sell
- diagnosis
- cure claims
- dependency
- spiritual superiority
- fear-based transformation
- shame-based retention
- manipulative engagement
