# User Identity Evolution

> **Phase:** B1 · **Mode:** GOVERNANCE / POSITIONING ONLY (no runtime code).
> **Governed by:** MMHB v7.4 Archival Kernel. Educational only — no diagnosis. Trauma-informed, consent-based, non-coercive. Crisis routing preserved. Business/brand domain — never contaminates healing flows.

This document defines **before/after identity states** as compassionate, self-authored descriptions of how a person may come to see themselves — never as labels imposed on them.

---

## 1. Framing law

Identity statements are **invitations the person may or may not claim**, written in their potential voice. MMHB never *tells* a person who they are, never diagnoses an identity, and never shames a "before" state. The "before" is honored as a valid place to be.

---

## 2. Before / after identity states

| Dimension | Before (honored, never shamed) | After (possible, self-claimed) |
|---|---|---|
| **Self-relationship** | "I'm my own harshest critic." | "I'm learning to be on my own side." |
| **Emotional weather** | "My feelings flood me." | "I can name what I feel and find my way back to calm." |
| **Belonging** | "I'm alone in this." | "I have support, and I'm allowed to reach for it." |
| **Agency** | "Nothing changes; I'm stuck." | "I notice my patterns and make small, aligned choices." |
| **Worth** | "I'm broken / too much." | "I'm a whole person who is growing." |
| **Tech trust** | "Apps just use me." | "This is a space that respects me." |

> Every "after" is **confidence-honest** and **non-guaranteed** — a possibility the person authors, not a result the platform claims.

---

## 3. Identity evolution principles

- **Non-coercive** — no "become your best self or else" pressure; rest and plateaus are valid identity states too.
- **Self-authored** — the person's own words define their evolution (ties to TI reflection, `../transformation/transformationSignals.md`).
- **Spiral, not ladder** — identity returns to themes at new depth; there is no "final self."
- **Trauma-informed** — the "before" is met with compassion, never used to manufacture inadequacy.
- **Reversible & private** — identity reflections are the person's own; never sold, ranked, or compared.

---

## 4. Anti-manipulation guardrails (identity-specific)

- No manufactured inadequacy ("you're not enough yet, buy X").
- No idealized-self pressure or comparison to other people.
- No identity claims used as conversion levers (Primary Law: business out of healing).
- No permanent labeling — the person can revise or release any framing.
