# Revenue Launch Readiness Checklist

## Must Exist Before Selling
- clear offer
- clear price
- clear audience
- clear promise
- clear disclaimer
- clear checkout path
- clear refund policy
- clear provider-informed boundary
- clear privacy statement
- clear transformation outcome

## First Sellable Product
7-Day Self-Understanding Reset

## Launch Rule
Do not sell vague enlightenment. Sell practical emotional clarity, calm, reflection, and self-understanding.

## Required Trust Boundaries
- no diagnosis
- no cure claims
- no crisis replacement
- no provider impersonation
- no dependency language
