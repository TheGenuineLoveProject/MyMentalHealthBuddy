# Non-Manipulative Marketing Rules

## Allowed
- clarity
- education
- invitation
- empowerment
- honest benefits
- provider-informed trust
- measurable self-reflection progress

## Not Allowed
- false scarcity
- emotional coercion
- exaggerated guarantees
- spiritual superiority
- medical promises
- crisis conversion funnels
- pain exploitation

## Core Principle
Sell practical transformation support, not fantasy, fear, or dependency.
