# Welcome Newsletter Template

**Pillar**: Orientation
**Trigger**: After newsletter signup (manual send only)
**Word count**: 200-350 words

---

## Subject Line
"Welcome. You're here, and that's enough."

## Preview Text
"A quiet space for self-awareness, emotional resilience, and personal growth."

---

## Structure

### Opening
Thank the reader for signing up. Set expectations immediately: low frequency, high care, no manipulation.

### What to Expect (3 bullets)
1. Low frequency, high care — we send when meaningful, not on a schedule
2. No manipulation — no countdown timers, no fake urgency, no guilt
3. Your privacy matters — we never sell data, unsubscribe anytime

### What You Can Try (3 links)
- Mood Check-In → /tools
- Journal Prompts → /journal
- Daily Wisdom → /reflection

### Safety Note
"This platform is for educational and emotional wellness purposes only. If you need immediate support, visit /crisis or call 988."

### Closing
"You do not need to be broken to be here. You do not need a reason. You are welcome exactly as you are."

### Footer
Use the crisis-safe footer (see crisis-safe-footer.md).

---

## Tone Checks Before Sending
- [ ] Does this feel like a warm welcome, not a sales pitch?
- [ ] No urgency or pressure to engage?
- [ ] Crisis resources included?
- [ ] Unsubscribe clearly available?
