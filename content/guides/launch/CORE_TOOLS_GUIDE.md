# Core Tools Guide

## Calm Me Down
Purpose: quick nervous system regulation.
Best for: anxiety, panic, overwhelm.
Tool: box breathing.

## Help Me Think Clearly
Purpose: reduce distorted or looping thoughts.
Best for: overthinking, self-criticism, confusion.
Tool: thought reframe.

## Understand This Feeling
Purpose: emotional clarity and self-awareness.
Best for: sadness, anger, numbness, overwhelm.
Tool: emotion check-in.
