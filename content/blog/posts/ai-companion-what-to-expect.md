We believe in being honest about what AI can and cannot do.

The Genuine Love Project uses AI to support reflection, offer perspective, and hold space for your thoughts. It is trained to listen, ask gentle questions, and help you explore your inner world with patience.

## What the AI companion can do

- Reflect your words back to you in ways that might reveal new patterns
- Offer journal prompts tailored to what you share
- Provide grounded, calm responses to difficult emotions
- Sit with you without rushing toward solutions

## What the AI companion cannot do

- Replace a therapist, counselor, or trained professional
- Identify or label mental health conditions
- Provide crisis intervention
- Guarantee emotional outcomes
- Understand the full complexity of your lived experience

## Why this matters

AI is a tool. A genuinely useful one, when built with care. But it has limits, and we would rather be honest about them than let you believe otherwise.

We built this companion to be a gentle starting point — not a final destination. If you ever feel like you need more support than a digital tool can offer, we actively encourage you to seek professional care.

## A note about privacy

Your conversations with the AI are private. We do not use them for advertising, sell them to third parties, or display them publicly. You can learn more on our [About page](/about).

## If you need immediate support

Our AI companion is not equipped to handle crisis situations. If you or someone you know needs immediate help, please visit our [Crisis Resources page](/crisis) or call 988.
