# Weekly Reflection Newsletter Template

**Pillar**: Reflection
**Frequency**: Weekly (manual send only)
**Word count**: 250-400 words

---

## Subject Line Pattern
[A gentle observation or question — no urgency, no clickbait]

Examples:
- "You don't have to fix everything today."
- "What would gentleness look like right now?"
- "A quiet thought for your week."

## Preview Text Pattern
[One sentence that adds context without pressure]

Examples:
- "A gentle reminder that rest is part of the work."
- "Something to sit with — no action required."

---

## Structure

### Opening (2-3 sentences)
A calm, grounded observation. Not advice. Not instruction. Just a thought to sit with.

### Body (150-250 words)
Explore the theme gently. Use "you might" language, not "you should." Include one small, optional practice if appropriate.

### Closing (1-2 sentences)
A warm sign-off that doesn't create obligation to return.

### Optional Link (1 max)
Choose one:
- /journal — "If you'd like to write about this"
- /reflection — "A quiet space to sit with this"
- /blog — "More on this theme"
- /crisis — Only when the topic is emotionally heavy

### Footer
Use the crisis-safe footer (see crisis-safe-footer.md).

---

## Tone Checks Before Sending
- [ ] Would this feel okay to read on a hard day?
- [ ] Is it free of urgency, guilt, or pressure?
- [ ] Does the reader feel free to delete this email?
- [ ] No medical claims or healing promises?
- [ ] CTA is invitational, not demanding?

---

*Manual send only. No auto-campaigns. Admin triggers test-send to self first.*
